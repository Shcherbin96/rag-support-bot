# Rewards Program

## How you earn points
You earn **5% back in points** on every purchase. 100 points are worth **$1**.
Points are added to your account 14 days after your order is delivered.

## How to redeem points
You can use points to pay for up to **30%** of your next order.

## Expiration
Points are valid for 12 months from the date they are earned, after which they expire.

## Enrollment
A rewards account is created automatically with your first order, linked to your email address.
