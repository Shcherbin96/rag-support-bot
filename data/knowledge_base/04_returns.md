# Returns and Exchanges

## Returning an item in good condition
You can return an item within **30 days** of delivery if it:
- has not been used,
- keeps its original condition and packaging,
- has proof of purchase (receipt or order number).

Refunds are issued to your original payment method within **5–10 business days** after we receive the return.

## Defective or damaged items
If an item arrives defective or broken, we offer a free exchange or return. We cover return shipping with a prepaid label.

## What cannot be returned
Personal-care items and certain textiles cannot be returned once their packaging is opened.

## How to start a return
Contact support with your order number and the reason. We will send instructions and a prepaid return label.
