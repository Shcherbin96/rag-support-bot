# About Nestwell

Nestwell is an online home goods store. We have been in business since 2019 and ship across the continental United States.

## What we sell
- Cookware and kitchen tools
- Home textiles (bedding, towels, throws)
- Lighting (lamps, floor lamps, ceiling lights)
- Small kitchen appliances
- Storage and organization products
- Home décor

## Why customers choose us
- Over 8,000 products in stock
- Nationwide shipping across the continental US
- Free standard shipping on orders over $49
- A rewards program that earns points on every order
- Support 7 days a week, 9 AM to 6 PM Central Time
