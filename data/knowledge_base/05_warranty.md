# Warranty

## Warranty length
- **Small appliances** — 12 months from the date of purchase.
- **Lighting** — 6 months.
- **Cookware, textiles, and décor** — no warranty, but the right to return defective items still applies.

## How to use the warranty
You will need your order number or receipt. Contact support with a description of the problem and photos, and we will open a warranty claim.

## What the warranty does not cover
Damage caused by the user, wear of consumable parts, and misuse.
