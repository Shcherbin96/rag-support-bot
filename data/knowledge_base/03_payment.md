# Payment

## Payment methods
- **Credit and debit cards** — Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.
- **Digital wallets** — PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
- **Pay at pickup** — cash or card when you collect a store-pickup order.
- **Buy now, pay later** — split your order into 4 interest-free installments paid every 2 weeks.

## Receipts and invoices
An electronic receipt is emailed to you after payment. Businesses can pay by invoice — contact support to set this up.

## Security
Payments are processed through a PCI-compliant payment provider. Your card details are never stored on our servers.
