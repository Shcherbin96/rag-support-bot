# Promotions and Discounts

## New customer discount
Use code **WELCOME10** for **10% off** your first order. Enter it at checkout; valid on orders over $15.

## Seasonal sales
We run regular sales with discounts up to 50% off. Watch for them in our email newsletter.

## Limits
- One promo code per order (codes cannot be combined).
- Promo codes do not apply to items already on sale.
- You can still use rewards points on a discounted order.
